By the end of this page you'll have joined your team, completed your profile, and RSVPed to your next event.

## Before you start

- You need a Discord account.
- Your captain or admin needs to have sent you an **invite link**. If you don't have one, ask in your team's Discord.

## Steps

<Steps>

1. **Open the invite link.**

   Click the URL your captain sent. You'll land on the Sideline web app.

2. **Sign in with Discord.**

   Click **Sign in** and authorise Sideline. We ask for your basic Discord profile — nothing more. The app redirects you back when you're done.

3. **Complete your profile.**

   Add your real name (so captains recognise you) and optionally a jersey number. You can edit this later from the **Profile** menu.

4. **Find your team's events.**

   The dashboard shows upcoming events for the teams you belong to. Each event has a date, time, location, and current RSVP count.

5. **RSVP.**

   Click **Yes**, **No**, or **Coming later**. Your captain sees the response immediately. You can change it any time until the event starts.

</Steps>

## Doing it from Discord instead

Everything above also works inside Discord:

- Upcoming events are posted in your own private **personal events channel** with the same RSVP buttons.
- Click a button — that's it. You don't need to open the web app at all for day-to-day use.
- Run `/complete` to complete your profile — name, date of birth, gender, and jersey number — right from Discord, ephemerally, as an alternative to the web onboarding form. See [Discord integration](/guides/discord-integration/#slash-commands) for details.
