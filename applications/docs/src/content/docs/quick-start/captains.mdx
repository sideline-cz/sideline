By the end of this page you'll have a working roster, a recurring training schedule, and a sent invite for new members.

## Before you start

- Your admin needs to have created the team and given you the **captain** role.
- You need to be signed in to Discord and to the Sideline web app.

## Steps

<Steps>

1. **Open your team.**

   From the dashboard, click your team name. You'll land on the team overview with tabs for Events, Roster, and Settings.

2. **Review the roster.**

   Check who's currently listed. Remove anyone who's no longer playing (archive rather than delete — it preserves attendance history). Add jersey numbers if your sport uses them.

3. **Create your groups.**

   If your team has subdivisions (first team / reserves / junior / senior), create a group per subdivision from the **Roster** tab and assign members to them. Events can target a group instead of the whole team.

4. **Create a recurring event.**

   Go to **Events → New event series**. Pick a day of the week, a start time, a location, and a training type. Sideline will post each occurrence in Discord at the right time and collect RSVPs.

5. **Send invites.**

   Go to **Roster → Invites → New invite**. Create one invite per group if you have subdivisions — new members are automatically placed in the right group. Copy each link and share it in your Discord. New players sign in with Discord and land straight on your team's roster.

   If you have a welcome channel configured in Team settings, the bot posts a personalised welcome embed when each new member joins the Discord server.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip" title="Before your first event">
  Check **Notifications** in the team settings. You can control when RSVP reminders go out and who gets pinged. Defaults are reasonable, but your team may want earlier or more frequent nudges.
</Aside>

## Day-to-day as captain

- **Before each event**, Sideline auto-posts the RSVP prompt in Discord. You don't need to do anything.
- **Missing RSVPs**? Trigger a reminder from the event page, or let the automatic reminder handle it.
- **One-off changes** (cancelling a training, moving locations)? Edit the individual event — not the series — so only that occurrence changes.
