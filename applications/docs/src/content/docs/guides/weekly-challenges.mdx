Weekly challenges give your team a shared goal for the week — a throwing drill, a fitness activity, or any other team-defined task. Captains set the challenge; members tick it off when they complete it.

## Where to find the challenges page

Go to **Team → Weekly challenges** in the left sidebar. The URL is `/teams/{teamId}/challenges`.

The page shows a grid of the last 12 weeks. On desktop, weeks run left-to-right with the most recent week on the left and a sticky first column showing member names. On mobile, each challenge appears as a vertical card.

## For captains — managing challenges

### Create a challenge

<Steps>

1. Click **Nová týdenní výzva** (New weekly challenge) at the top of the challenges page.
2. Choose the target week. You can set a challenge up to one week in the past or one week ahead of the current week.
3. Choose the **Druh** (kind): **Házecí** (throwing) or **Sportovní** (sport).
4. Enter a **title** (required, max 120 characters).
5. Optionally add a **description** (max 2000 characters).
6. Click **Save**.

</Steps>

Only one challenge can exist per team per week. If a challenge already exists for the chosen week, the form shows an error.

### Edit a challenge

Click **Upravit text** (Edit text) next to a challenge to update its title and description. The kind and week cannot be changed after creation.

### Delete a challenge

Click **Smazat výzvu** (Delete challenge) and confirm. Deleting a challenge removes all member completion records for that week as well.

<Aside type="caution">
Deletion is permanent. All completion data for the challenge is also removed.
</Aside>

### Discord announcements

When a challenge is created, the bot schedules a Discord embed to be posted at **09:00** in your team's configured timezone on the Monday the challenge starts. The embed shows the challenge title, kind, and the week's date range.

Configure the delivery channel in **Team settings → Discord integration → Weekly challenge channel**.

## For members — marking challenges complete

The current week's challenge column is highlighted. To mark your own completion:

1. Find your row in the grid (or your card on mobile).
2. Click the tick cell in the current week's column. Your cell updates to show **Splněno ✓**.

To undo, click the completed cell again.

<Aside>
You can only mark or unmark the **current week's** challenge. Past and future weeks are read-only. The current week is calculated using your team's configured timezone.
</Aside>

## Frequently asked questions

**Can a captain mark completion on behalf of a member?**

No. Each member marks their own completion. Completion is personal — it reflects the member's own effort.

**What happens to old challenges?**

Completed challenges stay in the grid for the last 12 weeks. Older challenges roll off the visible window automatically. Deleting a challenge removes it immediately regardless of age.

**Can there be more than one challenge per week?**

No. At most one challenge per team per week is allowed.
