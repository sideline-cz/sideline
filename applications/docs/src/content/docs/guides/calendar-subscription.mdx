Sideline exposes a per-user iCal feed so your phone or laptop calendar can show your team's events and upcoming payment due dates alongside the rest of your life.

## What is in the feed?

Your personal iCal feed contains two types of entries:

- **Team events** — every event you are invited to across all of your teams (training, matches, tournaments, etc.).
- **Payment due dates** — one all-day event per unpaid or overdue fee assignment, with a built-in alarm that fires one day before the due date. Assignments that are paid or waived are excluded. Events older than 180 days are not included.

## Getting your feed URL

<Aside type="note">
A full how-to guide for subscribing in Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, and Outlook is coming soon. The steps below give you the URL you need.
</Aside>

The feed URL is unique to your account and acts as a private credential — anyone who has it can read your events.

1. Log in to the Sideline web app.
2. Open **Profile → Calendar** (or navigate to `/me/ical`).
3. Copy the `webcal://` URL shown on the page.
4. Paste it into your calendar application's "Subscribe to calendar" dialog.

If you believe the URL has been shared or compromised, click **Regenerate** to get a new one. The old URL stops working immediately.

## Payment events in your calendar

Each unpaid or overdue fee assignment appears as an all-day event on its due date. The event title follows the format:

- `Payment due — <Fee name> (<outstanding amount> <currency>)` for pending or partial assignments
- `[Overdue] Payment due — <Fee name> (<outstanding amount> <currency>)` for overdue assignments

A built-in alarm fires one day before the due date so your calendar app can notify you in advance.

<Aside type="tip">
Payment calendar events are a complement to Discord reminder DMs, not a replacement. See [Payment reminders](/guides/finances/#payment-reminders) for details on the DM cadence.
</Aside>
