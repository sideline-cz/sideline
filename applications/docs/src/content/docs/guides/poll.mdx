The poll feature lets captains post interactive polls directly in Discord. Members vote using buttons, and the board updates in real time with counts and percentages.

## Creating a poll

Only members with the `poll:manage` permission (Admin and Captain by default) can create polls.

<Steps>

1. Go to the Discord channel where you want the poll to appear.
2. Type `/anketa` (or `/poll` in English) and press Enter.
3. Fill in the required options:
   - **question** — the poll question (up to 300 characters).
   - **options** — the choices, separated by semicolons, e.g. `Pizza; Sushi; Tacos`. You need at least 2 and at most 10 options. Duplicate options are not allowed.
4. Optionally configure:
   - **multiple** — set to `True` to let members vote for more than one option (default: single-choice).
   - **deadline** — an optional closing time in the team's timezone, format `YYYY-MM-DD HH:mm`. After the deadline the poll closes automatically on the next interaction.
   - **allowed_role** — a Discord role whose members are allowed to add new options. Leave blank to allow only the creator and captains to add options.
5. The bot posts a public **Poll** embed in the channel with vote buttons. Only you see the ephemeral "poll created" confirmation — the embed itself is visible to everyone.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note" title="One poll per command">
  Each `/anketa` invocation creates a separate poll. There is no automatic cleanup of old polls.
</Aside>

## Voting

Any team member can vote by clicking the option buttons on the poll embed.

- **Single-choice polls** — tap an option button to vote. Tapping the same option again retracts your vote. Tapping a different option moves your vote.
- **Multiple-choice polls** — tap any number of option buttons to toggle them on or off.

The poll embed updates immediately for everyone, showing the current vote counts and percentages.

<Aside type="note" title="Vote confirmation">
  After voting you receive an ephemeral (private) confirmation message describing what changed (vote counted, moved, retracted, added, or removed).
</Aside>

## Adding options

Members can suggest new options by clicking **➕ Add option** on the open poll embed.

<Steps>

1. Click **➕ Add option**.
2. A small form appears. Enter the option label (up to 80 characters).
3. Click **Submit**. The new option is added to the poll and the embed updates.

</Steps>

Who can add options depends on the `allowed_role` setting:

- If **no role is set**, only the poll creator and team members with `poll:manage` can add options.
- If a **role is set**, any member who has that Discord role can add options (in addition to captains/admins).

Duplicate labels (case-insensitive) and labels longer than 80 characters are rejected. The poll can hold at most 10 options in total.

## Removing options

Members with the `poll:manage` permission (Admin and Captain by default) can remove options from an open poll by clicking **➖ Remove option** on the embed.

<Steps>

1. Click **➖ Remove option** on the open poll embed.
2. An ephemeral (private) drop-down appears listing the current options, each labeled with its regional-indicator letter (🇦, 🇧, 🇨…) and text.
3. Select one or more options you want to remove. You can select at most *total options minus 2* at once — the poll must always keep at least 2 options.
4. Submit. The selected options are removed and the poll embed updates for everyone.

</Steps>

<Aside type="caution" title="Votes are lost on removal">
  All votes cast for a removed option are permanently deleted. The remaining options are renumbered so their 🇦🇧🇨 letters stay consecutive — for example, if you remove option 🇧, the former 🇨 becomes the new 🇧.
</Aside>

<Aside type="note" title="Minimum 2 options">
  If a poll already has exactly 2 options, the **➖ Remove option** button returns an error. Close the poll instead.
</Aside>

## Seeing who voted

Any team member can see who voted for each option by clicking **👥 Who voted?** on the poll embed. The button is available on both open and closed polls.

The bot replies with a private (ephemeral) message — visible only to you — that lists each option with:

- The option label and its current vote count.
- The names of members who voted for it, formatted as `Name (@mention)` (no actual Discord pings are sent).

If more than 60 people voted for the same option, the list shows the first 60 voters and a "…and N more" note.

<Aside type="note" title="Private view">
  The voter breakdown is always ephemeral — only you see it after clicking. This lets captains review results without broadcasting them to the channel.
</Aside>

## Closing a poll

Members with the `poll:manage` permission (Admin and Captain by default) can close a poll by clicking **🔒 Close poll** on the embed.

- Closing is permanent — the poll can no longer receive votes or new options.
- The embed updates to show a grey "closed" state with the final results, including the winner (or a tie/empty notice).
- The vote and close buttons are removed; the **👥 Who voted?** button remains so members can still inspect the final voter breakdown.

<Aside type="note" title="Lazy deadline close">
  If you set a deadline, the poll does not close automatically on a timer. Instead, it closes on the next interaction (vote or add option attempt) after the deadline has passed. The interacting member will see a "poll has closed" notice.
</Aside>

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Likely cause |
|---------|--------------|
| `/anketa` does not appear | You do not have the `poll:manage` permission. Ask your captain or admin. |
| "Provide between 2 and 10 options" error | You entered fewer than 2 or more than 10 semicolon-separated options. |
| "Duplicate options are not allowed" error | Two or more options have the same label (ignoring case). |
| "Each option must be at most 80 characters" error | At least one option is too long. |
| "Invalid deadline format" error | The deadline must be in `YYYY-MM-DD HH:mm` format. |
| "The deadline must be in the future" error | The deadline you entered has already passed. |
| "Only captains and admins can do that" error | You tried to close the poll, add an option, or remove an option without the required permission. |
| "This poll has already been closed" notice | The poll's deadline passed or it was manually closed. |
| Cannot add an option | The poll has reached the 10-option limit, or you do not have the required role/permission. |
| **➖ Remove option** returns "minimum 2 options" | The poll already has exactly 2 options and cannot be reduced further. Close it instead. |
| **➖ Remove option** is missing | The poll is closed, or you do not have the `poll:manage` permission. |
